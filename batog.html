Step 1 Define the terms below:
i. Gene: is a unit of heredity/genetic material composed of DNA, located on the chromosomes

ii. Hybrid: is an offspring formed from the crossing of parents that are genetically different but are of the same species. Example: white and red flower has been used as an illustration

iii. Traits: is a feature or character possessed by an organism that can be transferred from parents to their children. Examples of inherited traits are; eye color, rolling tongue, widows peak, length of second toes, gaps between teeth etc

iv. Heredity: is a process in which parents passes certain genes to their children. Example: possibility of parents inheriting Hemophilia

v. Genotype: is referred to the genetic make-up of an individual. Example of genotype in human is blood group

Diagram shows pictures of blood groups in human

vi. Phenotype: is referred to as the observable traits (physical characteristics that can be seen). Example: ear wax, eye type, eye color, hair color, flower color etc
